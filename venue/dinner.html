Newcomer Dinner

{% comment %}

When: Monday, March 6th

The Newcomer Dinner is an opportunity for veterans of the code4lib conference to meet and welcome first-timers. Attendees sign up to join others for dinner the night before the main sessions of conference begin. The conference organizers have created a list of potential restaurants, which you can browse below. You can then sign up for a restaurant (or even add a new venue) using the Google Doc Sign Up Sheet.

Sign-up for the Newcomer Dinner

To get a sense of the locations and neighborhoods, you can check out the Activities Map.

{% endcomment %}

Transportation

{% comment %}

Attendees must arrange for their own transportation to the restaurants. Some of them are within walking distance. For those that are not, we recommend carpooling with a local, calling a taxi, using a ride share service, or taking the bus. Below are some public transit options for the various neighborhoods noted in the restaurant list.

Westwood

Several of the restaurants in Westwood are within walking distance of the conference center. However, if you decide to dine at one of the locations further south, you will want to take the Big Blue Bus.

Travel Time: Approximately 5-20 minutes (walking or bus)

Lines:

  • Big Blue Bus: Line 1
  • Big Blue Bus Rapid 12 (to get further south on Westwood Blvd.)

Directions: Walk approximately .5 miles to board at Westwood Blvd and Weyburn Avenue. For the return trip, deboard at Westwood Blvd and Le Conte (pay close attention to when the last bus stops in Westwood. It is different for each system and each line within the system).

Fare: $1.25 (each way)

Sawtelle & Brentwood

Travel Time: Approximately 15 min. (Taxi) / 25 min. (Bus)

If you are heading toward Sawtelle or Brentwood via bus, you will want to take the Big Blue Bus: Line 1 or Culver City Bus: Line 6. These will take you close to Sawtelle, with a short walk to reach the dining area.

Big Blue Bus: Line 1

$1.25 (each way)
