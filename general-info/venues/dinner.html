Newcomer Dinner

When: Tuesday, February 13th

The Newcomer Dinner is an opportunity for veterans of the code4lib conference to meet and welcome first-timers. Attendees sign up to join others for dinner the night before the main sessions of conference begin. The conference organizers will provide created a list of potential restaurants soon.

Transportation

Attendees must arrange for their own transportation to the restaurants. Some of them are within walking distance. For those that are not, we recommend carpooling with a local, calling a taxi, using a ride share service, or taking the bus or metro.
